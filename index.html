What are phrasal verbs?


Phrasal verbs are made up of 1 or more parts:
Verb + particle: turn + off = make an appliance or machine stop running.


Phrasal verbs change the original meaning of the verb. Eg: turn = a change of direction when moving.


There are over 5,000 phrasal verbs in English and many have multiple meanings. English language learners should not try to learn off lists of these verbs by heart. The best way to learn phrasal verbs is to study them in context.
